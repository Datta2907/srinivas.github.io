Lost & Found App - HCC Project

Overview

The Lost & Found App is designed to help students and campus members report lost items and find belongings easily. The goal is to create a user-friendly platform where people can quickly post lost or found items, search by category or location, and securely contact others to retrieve their belongings.

Key Features

  • Post lost items with details and images
  • Search and filter items by category or location
  • Submit Found items to the nearest facility available
  • Admin moderation and verification
